### EDITING SPECIFICATIONS - VIDEO 1

**PACING & CUTS**
- Hook (0-3s): 1 cut (no transitions, hard cut to script)
- Problem section (3-10s): 4 cuts = 1 cut every 1.75s (fast, attention-grabbing)
- Solution section (10-35s): 7 cuts = 1 cut every 3.5s (slightly slower, showing results)
- Promise section (35-50s): 5 cuts = 1 cut every 3s (emotional, inspiring)
- CTA section (50-60s): 3 cuts = 1 cut every 3.3s (clear, direct)
- **Rule:** Minimum 1 jump cut every 2-3 seconds (MrBeast standard)

**TRANSITIONS**
- Hook to Problem: Hard cut (no transition)
- Problem to Solution: Crossfade (0.2s) with whoosh SFX
- Solution to Promise: Crossfade (0.3s) with uplifting music swell
- Promise to CTA: Hard cut (emphasize direct call-to-action)
- No slide/wipe transitions (too dated)

**COLOR GRADING**
- Hook/Problem: Cool tones (slight blue shift), slightly desaturated to show struggle
- Solution: Warm up colors, increase saturation as BDNF activates
- Promise: Warm, vibrant, high saturation (energy and vitality)
- CTA: Neutral, slightly bright (clear information presentation)
- Overall: High contrast, punchy, social media optimized

**TEXT OVERLAY TIMING**
- Hook text: 2 seconds on screen
- Problem text: 1.5-2 seconds each
- Solution text: 2-2.5 seconds each
- Promise text: 2.5-3 seconds each
- CTA text: 3+ seconds (time to read and click)
- All text: White, Bold sans-serif, 40-60px size for mobile viewing

**AUDIO LEVELS**
- Voiceover: -3 to -6 dB (clear, primary)
- Music: -12 to -15 dB (supportive, under voiceover)
- SFX: -9 to -12 dB (noticeable but not overwhelming)
- Use gentle compression to smooth audio, avoid harsh peaks

**EXPORT SETTINGS**
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080) for YouTube
- Frame Rate: 30fps (24fps acceptable for cinematic feel)
- Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps (YouTube optimized)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (standard YouTube)
